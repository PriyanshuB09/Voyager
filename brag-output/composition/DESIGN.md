## Direction

Mission-control precision. The real Voyager interface is enlarged for video rather than redesigned: dense field imagery, path geometry, branching nodes, compact technical metadata, and a restrained teal/cyan glow. Rounded panels retain Voyager's 12–16px character; headings grow to cinematic scale while labels remain clearly legible.

## Motion

Mechanical and deliberate: scan, draw, lock, connect, publish. Use transform/opacity motion, SVG path drawing, short camera pushes, and finite ambient breathing. Major moments align to the bundled music cues at 8.74s, 13.11s, and 17.47s.

## Constraints

- Keep the real FRC field artwork visible in the product scenes.
- No generic abstract technology imagery.
- No fake metrics.
- Preserve exact Voyager UI labels.
- Text contrast must meet Hyperframes validation.
